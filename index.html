A MODERN CALENDER

Please read three sections before going ahead

A calendar year is a one-year period that begins on January 1 and ends on December 31, based on the commonly-used Gregorian calendar.
Calendars are useful tools for keeping track of upcoming meetings, deadlines, and milestones. They can help you visualize your schedule and remind you of important events, such as holidays and vacation time.

DAY-TO-DATE

In DAY-TO-DATE,you can get the dates of the day in the month of the year. At first,enter the year(1700-2100) in input box and you have to select the day and month before pressing the button otherwise it will automatically assume january and sunday.you will be able to see the dates after pressing the button named "show".

DATE-TO-DAY

In DATE-TO-DAY,you can get days of date in month of year. At first,enter the year(1700-2100) in both input box and you have to select the day and month and date before pressing the button other wise it will automatically assume january, 1 and sunday.you will be able to see the dates after pressing the button named "show". in this feature,you can also see the total number of days of the particular month of the years.

DAY-IN-YEAR

In DAY-IN-YEAR,you can get days of date of years. At first,enter the year(1700-2100) in input box and you have to select the date before pressing the button other wise it will automatically assume "1".you will be able to see the days after pressing the button named "show".
